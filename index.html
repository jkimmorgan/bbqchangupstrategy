13VIP Drop · How It Actually Works
bb.q × DOORDASH · COACHELLA 2027

VIP Drop runs on existing Coco infrastructure relocated 130 miles east — operating in a 2-acre paved compound DoorDash already engineers around.

The operational mechanics

  • BBQ kitchen tent at compound perimeter — adjacent to artist trailer access road.
  • 30–50 Coco robots relocated from LA fleet via flatbed truck (5-hr drive). Standard fleet relocation, no new build.
  • 4–6 charging stations at compound perimeter (existing Coco station design).
  • VIP compound = 2–5 acres of paved walkways behind main stage. Coco's native operating environment.
  • Order via DoorDash app from VIP guest's phone → BBQ kitchen → Coco loads → 5–8 min delivery to GPS-tagged endpoint.
  • Operating hours: 11am–2am across 2 weekends. ~14 hr/day × 6 days = 84 operating hours.

Coco fleet utilization math

  • 40 robots × 84 hours × ~3 deliveries/hr = capacity for ~10K deliveries across festival window.
  • Target: 1,200–2,000 actual VIP deliveries. Easy capacity headroom — robots stress-tested at 70% utilization, not 100%.

Why this is operationally low-risk

Controlled environment

VIP compound has controlled access perimeter, paved walkways, no public crowds, no airspace concerns, no pedestrian density issues. Coco's exact operating envelope.

Existing tech, existing partner

Not a new technology pilot. Coco has done 100K+ deliveries in LA, Chicago, Miami already. This is a relocation of existing infrastructure for 6 days.

Climate-hardened

Coco operates in LA summer (95°F+) routinely. Indio April average is 87°F. Temperature is not the risk; battery cycling is — addressed by 4-6 charging station design.

The DoorDash CFO answer

The VIP Drop is not a stunt. It's a strategic deployment of existing Coco infrastructure that produces $2-3M of equivalent R&D data on extreme-condition fleet operations, while also generating Coachella-grade celebrity content for the brand-marketing P&L.

Source: Coco Robotics technical specs and operational data (LA fleet); Coachella VIP compound architecture; DoorDash Coco-relocation logistics protocols.
